About Cindy

Cindy Gabriela is a passionate beginner chef specializing in American, Latin, and Italian cuisines. Based in Houston, Texas, she focuses on creating flavorful, authentic dishes with a modern twist. She is particularly known for her American Texas-style cooking.

Specialties

American Texas BBQ

Grilled meats, BBQ ribs, and Texas-style brisket are Cindy's signature dishes, embodying the flavors of the South.

Latin Cuisine

Spicy and flavorful, Cindy brings the best of Latin-inspired dishes to the table, from tacos to empanadas.

Italian Classics

Delicious Italian pastas and pizzas, all made from scratch, are key to Cindy's Italian food offerings.
